Bio

I'm a designer, entrepreneur, and systems thinker with a passion for creating solutions at the intersection of design, technology, and business. My background in Design & Innovation Management at Oregon State University revolves around Design Thinking: a human-centered approach that uses empathy, creativity, and the iterative process to solve real-world problems. Major themes in my work include holistic design, co-creation, and ethnography.

I'm co-founder and creative director of Cascade Creatives, a design agency that serves small businesses, organizations, and non-profits. Our mission is to provide organizations with custom, technological solutions that streamline their operations.
